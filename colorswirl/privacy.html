Color Swirl Privacy

Color Swirl tracks user's activity on the app to improve the user's experience and enhance its functionality. While some have little or no concern, others have concerns that need to be addressed.

Who has Access to Your Information?

As a part of my goal to keep everything secure, I have no access to your personal data. All of your account info is hashed and sent to a private database. You information is private and secure.

How is Your Information Used?

Your information is not visible to anyone. However, you app activity is shared anomously to reprt crashes, bugs, and possible suspicious activity.

Privacy is Personal

Not everyone has the same needs for their privacy. That's why on your account page you can manage what information is shared with the app. The account page will be where you manage your account and will be created when the update is released.
